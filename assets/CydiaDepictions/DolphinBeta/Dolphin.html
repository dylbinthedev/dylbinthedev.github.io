Works on iOS 12, 13 and 14. Has arm64 and arm64e support. Devices with A8 chip and below are unsupported.

Dolphin iOS (Beta) is a Gamecube/Wii Emulator which runs at full speed on modern devices.

Port of the original Dolphin Emulator, supports MFi controllers such as Dualshock 4 and Xbox One S controllers.
