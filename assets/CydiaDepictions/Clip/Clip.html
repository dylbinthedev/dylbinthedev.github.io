Works on iOS 12, 13 and 14. Has arm64 and arm64e support.

Clip is a clipboard manager for iOS that can run in the background indefinitely, listening for changes to the clipboard and saving your clippings for later use.

Clip can monitor clipboard even when Clip isn't opened, Save text, URLs and images copied to the clipboard.

It can also copy, delete and share clippings and has a customizable history limit.
